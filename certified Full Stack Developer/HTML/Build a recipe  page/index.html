COOKIES

"Because happiness is homemade... Let's bake the easiest cookies ever!"

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. In a mixing bowl, *combine* peanut butter, sugar, and egg until smooth and creamy
  3. Scoop small portions of dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  4. Gently press each cookie with a fork to flatten and make a criss-cross pattern
  5. Bake for *10–12 minutes* or until edges start turning golden brown
  6. Allow the cookies to *cool* before enjoying your delicious, homemade treats!
