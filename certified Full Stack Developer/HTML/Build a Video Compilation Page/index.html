UI/UX Designing

UI/UX design focuses on creating user-friendly and visually appealing digital experiences. UI (User Interface) deals with the look and layout—like colors, fonts, and buttons—while UX (User Experience) ensures the product is easy and satisfying to use. For freshers, it's important to learn design basics, user research, wireframing, and tools like Figma

Web UI/UX Design

Web UI/UX Design focuses on creating user-friendly, responsive, and visually appealing interfaces for websites that work seamlessly across browsers and devices.

Mobile UI/UX Design

Mobile UI/UX Design involves designing intuitive and efficient app experiences tailored to the constraints and capabilities of mobile devices.
